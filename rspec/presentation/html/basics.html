Mocks e Stubs

TO THE RESCUE!

  • MOCK => CLASSE do Paraguay.
  • STUB => METODO do Paraguay.

obj = mock(Modelo)

obj.stub!(:metodo)

obj.stub!(:metodo).and_return(x)
